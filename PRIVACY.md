# Privacy Policy

**Create Reminders App**  
*Last Updated: March 2026*

## Overview

Create Reminders App is an AI-powered reminder and task management tool that runs entirely in your browser within ChatGPT. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymous usage data including tool invocations, response times, and general device categories (iOS, Android, Desktop). We log which features are used (e.g., reminder creation, completion, filtering) but do not log your reminder text or task details.
- **Coarse Location & Locale**: When the widget runs inside ChatGPT, we may receive coarse location data (city, region, country, timezone) and locale setting via ChatGPT's `_meta` context. This data is used solely for aggregated analytics (e.g., understanding geographic usage patterns) and is not tied to individual identity.
- **Device & Browser Category**: We classify your device type (iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, etc.) from the user-agent string provided by ChatGPT. We do not store the raw user-agent string in tool responses.
- **Email Address (optional, newsletter only)**: If you voluntarily subscribe to updates via the in-widget "Notify Me" feature, your email address is collected and sent to our email service provider, Buttondown, for the sole purpose of delivering product updates. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.
- **Session Data**: Temporary session identifiers for MCP connection management. These are ephemeral and not persisted beyond the active session.
- **Error Logs**: Anonymous error information (error type, response time, device category) to improve reliability. Error logs do not contain user-generated content.

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Financial account credentials or banking information
- Social Security Numbers or government IDs
- Credit card or payment information
- Health information
- Precise location data (GPS coordinates, street addresses)
- Full conversation history or chat transcripts
- Raw user-agent strings in tool responses

## Data Processing

All reminder management is performed:
- **Client-side**: In your browser within the ChatGPT sandbox
- **Locally**: Your reminders are processed locally and stored in browser localStorage
- **Ephemerally**: Session data is not persisted on our servers beyond the active session

## Data Storage

- **Browser LocalStorage**: Your reminders and stats are cached in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it anytime with the Reset button.
- **Server Logs**: Anonymous analytics (feature usage counts, device categories, coarse location summaries) are logged for up to 30 days for service improvement, then automatically deleted.

## Third-Party Services

- **Buttondown** (email service): If you opt in to the newsletter, your email address is shared with Buttondown ([buttondown.email](https://buttondown.email)) solely to deliver product updates. No other personal data is shared with Buttondown. See [Buttondown's privacy policy](https://buttondown.email/legal/privacy).
- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties for marketing purposes.

## Data Retention

- **LocalStorage**: Persists until you clear it or use the Reset button
- **Server Logs**: Retained for up to 30 days, then automatically deleted
- **Newsletter Subscription**: Retained by Buttondown until you unsubscribe

## Your Rights

You can:
- Clear your browser's localStorage at any time to remove cached reminder data
- Use the app without providing any personal information
- Unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time
- Request deletion of your analytics data by contacting us

## Security

- All communications use HTTPS encryption
- The widget runs in a sandboxed iframe with strict Content Security Policy
- No sensitive financial data is transmitted or stored
- Tool responses are minimized to include only data necessary for widget functionality

## Children's Privacy

This service is suitable for all ages, including users aged 13–17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be noted in the app documentation.

## Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests:
- **Email**: support@layer3labs.io
- **Deletion requests**: Include the UTC timestamp of your ChatGPT session; we will delete associated logs within 7 business days.
